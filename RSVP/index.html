How It Works

Traditional reading involves constant eye movement (saccades). We eliminate that.

01

The Problem

Your eyes waste time moving left to right and scanning for new lines. This slows down processing.

02

The Fix

We flash words in the exact same spot. We highlight the Optimal Recognition Point (the middle letter).

03

The Result

Your brain recognizes the word instantly. Just relax your eyes and stare at the red letter.
